Medieval Malfunction

A little while ago I asked a buddy of mine if he wanted to join a Game jam. It ended up that we joined 2 and this is the result of the second jam we joined.

The theme for the Game jam was "to cheat" which I wanted to implement with codes to type in as powerups instead of just picking them up.

It is a rather short game, very cryptic according to the feedback we got, but might add and fix a little later on. But all graphics, music and code is made by us 2 under the time of 2 weeks.
